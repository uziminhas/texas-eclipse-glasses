### What is a Solar Eclipse?

A solar eclipse occurs when the Moon passes between the Sun and the Earth, casting a shadow on the Earth's surface. During this celestial phenomenon, the Moon partially or completely blocks the Sun, creating a breathtaking display in the sky.
